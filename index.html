About US

Auburn CSO is the largest student group at Auburn University and is one of the most influential Chinese student groups in U.S. Auburn CSO is dedicated to the goal of promoting social, intellectual and cultural activities for Chinese students and scholars at Auburn University and for other interested members of Auburn community.
